  • Getting Started
  • Fun Tools
  • Downloading Art Book Templates from DirectorZone
  • Printing and Settings

Getting Started


  • Select your drawing tool (chalk, pencil, marker or crayon) by touching/clicking it once on the bottom right.
  • Select a color to use from the available colors at the bottom (touch/click or or touch/click the library tray and then slide the colors to scroll through them).
  • Use the slider to adjust the line width.
  • Use the Fun Tools on the left hand side to add to your drawing.
  • Use the eraser tool to remove unwanted parts of your drawing.
  • Touch/Click to undo the last action performed.
  • Touch/Click to redo the previous action.
  • Select New for a clean drawing area.
  • Touch/Click Templates to select a coloring page to color (touch/click or or touch/click the library tray and then slide the pictures to scroll through them).
  • Touch/Click Imports and then select to import an image into your drawing so you can trace it or use it as a stamp.
  • Touch/Click My Drawings to open a previously saved drawing.

Fun Tools

Stamp

  • Touch/Click Templates to select from the available stamps (touch/click or or touch/click the library tray and then slide the stamps to scroll through them).
  • Touch/Click Imports and then select to import an image so you can use it as a stamp.
  • Select an image and then click to remove it from Imports.
  • Touch/Click the location on the drawing where you want to place the stamp (you can move, rotate or resize the stamp while the lock is displayed as ).
  • Use to resize the stamp.
  • Use to rotate the stamp.
  • Tap/Click to set the stamp in place and lock it .

Image Bloom

  • Touch/Click Templates to select from the available images (touch/click or or touch/click the library tray and then slide the images to scroll through them).
  • YouPaint will place different images randomly based on the image type you selected.
  • Use the slider to adjust the size of the image.
  • Touch/Click the location on the drawing where you want to place the image.
  • Drag your finger/mouse across the drawing to create a swirling chain-like effect by placing repeating images.

Tracing Paper

  • Within Templates select a picture to trace (touch/click or or touch/click the library tray and then slide the pictures to scroll through them).
  • Touch/Click Imports and then select to import an image so you can trace it.
  • Touch/Click My Drawings to open a previously saved drawing to trace it.
  • Select a drawing or image and then click to remove it from the YouPaint program.
  • Touch/Click to show/hide the tracing lines and see your drawing more clearly.

Paint Bucket

  • Select a color to use from the available colors at the bottom (touch/click or or touch/click the library tray and then slide the colors to scroll through them).
  • Touch/Click the location on the drawing you want to fill with color.
  • The paint bucket will fill each enclosed area that you touch/click with the selected color.

Art Book

  • Your Art Book contains Templates you can use for coloring, imported photos and graphics in Imports, and your previously created drawings in My Drawings.
  • Touch/Click the Save button to save your current drawing to your Art Book.
  • Touch/Click Templates to select a coloring page to color (touch/click or or touch/click the library tray and then slide the pictures to scroll through them).
  • Touch/Click Imports and then select to import an image into your drawing so you can trace it or use it as a stamp.
  • Touch/Click My Drawings to open a previously saved drawing.
  • Select a drawing or image and then click to remove it from the YouPaint program.
  • Touch/Click the closed Art Book at any time for quick access to Templates, Imports and My Drawings.

Downloading Art Book Templates from DirectorZone

You can expand your CyberLink YouPaint Art Book template library by downloading additional tracing paper and coloring pages from the free DirectorZone web service.

To download Art Book templates from DirectorZone, do this:

  1. Click on to enter the Art Book library, and then select the Templates tab.
  2. Click on the button in the templates tray. If this button is not displayed click the button to scroll all the way left to display it.
  3. The DirectorZone web site loads in your default web browser. You must first sign in to DirectorZone to download templates. If you don't have an account, click the Sign up link at the top of the page to get one for free.
  4. Select the YouPaint tab to display all the available tracing paper and coloring pages you can download.
  5. Find a template you want to download, and then click the Download link underneath the template.
  6. Click Download again.
  7. Save the template to your computer. Find the location on your computer where you saved the template .dzp file, and then double-click on it to install it in your library.

    8. Note: if you are downloading the template in Microsoft Internet Explorer, select Allow if you receive the Internet Explorer Security dialog during the installation process.

  8. A message displays indicating that the template was successfully installed and imported into your CyberLink YouPaint Art Book library.
  9. Click on OK to close the message, and go into the Art Book library to use the downloaded template.
    Note: if you clicked the Save button instead of Open in step 7, find the location on your computer where you saved the template .dzp file, and then double-click on it to install the template in your library.

Printing, Settings and other Options

  • Touch/Click Save As... to save your current drawing as a JPEG, PNG or BMP.
  • Touch/Click Set as Wallpaper to use the current drawing as your computer's desktop background. Next, select whether to Center, Tile or Stretch it on your desktop, and then touch/click Set as Wallpaper to apply it.
  • Touch/Click Email to email your current drawing to your friends or family, if you have a default email program defined in the Control Panel.
  • Touch/Click Print to print off your drawings. You can print them in color or in black and white, and also select a desired printing orientation.
  • Touch/Click Update to check if there are any updates or a new version of YouPaint available.
  • Select the printer you want to print your drawings on within Settings (touch/click or to scroll through the available printers).
  • Use the slider to adjust the sound effects volume within Settings.

  • Use the Fun Tools on the left hand side to add to your drawing.
  • Use the eraser tool to remove unwanted parts of your drawing.

  • Touch/Click to redo the previous action.
  • Select New for a clean drawing area.
  • Touch/Click Templates to select a coloring page to color (touch/click or or touch/click the library tray and then slide the pictures to scroll through them).
  • Touch/Click Imports and then select to import an image into your drawing so you can trace it or use it as a stamp.

Fun Tools

Stamp

  • Touch/Click Templates to select from the available stamps (touch/click or or touch/click the library tray and then slide the stamps to scroll through them).

Image Bloom

  • Touch/Click Templates to select from the available images (touch/click or or touch/click the library tray and then slide the images to scroll through them).

  • Touch/Click the location on the drawing where you want to place the image.

  • Select a color to use from the available colors at the bottom (touch/click or or touch/click the library tray and then slide the colors to scroll through them).

  • The paint bucket will fill each enclosed area that you touch/click with the selected color.

Art Book

  • Touch/Click Templates to select a coloring page to color (touch/click or or touch/click the library tray and then slide the pictures to scroll through them).

  • Touch/Click My Drawings to open a previously saved drawing.

Downloading Art Book Templates from DirectorZone

You can expand your CyberLink YouPaint Art Book template library by downloading additional tracing paper and coloring pages from the free DirectorZone web service.

To download Art Book templates from DirectorZone, do this:

  1. Click on to enter the Art Book library, and then select the Templates tab.
  2. Click on the button in the templates tray. If this button is not displayed click the button to scroll all the way left to display it.
  3. The DirectorZone web site loads in your default web browser. You must first sign in to DirectorZone to download templates. If you don't have an account, click the Sign up link at the top of the page to get one for free.
  4. Select the YouPaint tab to display all the available tracing paper and coloring pages you can download.
  5. Find a template you want to download, and then click the Download link underneath the template.
  6. Click Download again.
  7. Save the template to your computer. Find the location on your computer where you saved the template .dzp file, and then double-click on it to install it in your library.

    8. Note: if you are downloading the template in Microsoft Internet Explorer, select Allow if you receive the Internet Explorer Security dialog during the installation process.

  8. A message displays indicating that the template was successfully installed and imported into your CyberLink YouPaint Art Book library.
  9. Click on OK to close the message, and go into the Art Book library to use the downloaded template.
    Note: if you clicked the Save button instead of Open in step 7, find the location on your computer where you saved the template .dzp file, and then double-click on it to install the template in your library.

  • Touch/Click Save As... to save your current drawing as a JPEG, PNG or BMP.

  • Touch/Click Email to email your current drawing to your friends or family, if you have a default email program defined in the Control Panel.
  • Touch/Click Print to print off your drawings. You can print them in color or in black and white, and also select a desired printing orientation.
  • Touch/Click Update to check if there are any updates or a new version of YouPaint available.
  • Select the printer you want to print your drawings on within Settings (touch/click or to scroll through the available printers).
  • Use the slider to adjust the sound effects volume within Settings.
